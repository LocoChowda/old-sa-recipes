Spinach Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 big onions chopped
  • 4 beaten eggs
  • 3 tablespoons margerine
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • 500g chopped spinach
  • salt and pepper
  • Puff Pastry

Recipe instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C.
  2. Boil the spinach in salt water for 20 minutes, then drain.
  3. Fry the chopped onion in the margerine till golden.
  4. Add the cooked spinach, and the cheddar cheese and toss in the pot.
  5. Add the beaten eggs and salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Line an oven dish with puff pastry and pour in the pie filling.
  7. Cover with a layer of puf patry and brush with egg.
  8. Bake in the oven till golden brown.
