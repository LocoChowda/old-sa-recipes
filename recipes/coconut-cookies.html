Chewy Coconut Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 tin condensed milk
  • 200g coconut
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essense
  • a pinch of salt
  • cherries cut in ¼ to decorate

Recipe instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C.
  2. Grease the baking tray lightly with margerine.
  3. Mix all of the ingredients together.
  4. Spoon the mix onto the baking tray and bake for 10 minutes.
