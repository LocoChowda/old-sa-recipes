Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas
  • 2 well beaten eggs
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • ½ teaspoon Baking Powder
  • ¾ teaspoon Bicarb
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla essense
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Recipe instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mash the bananas, eggs, butter, milk and sugar together.
  3. Now work the rest of the ingredients into the mash.
  4. Rub margerine into your baking tin.
  5. Pour your banana mix into the baking tin and bake four 1 hour.
